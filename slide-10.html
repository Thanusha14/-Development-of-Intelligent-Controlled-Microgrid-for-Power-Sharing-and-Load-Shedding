METHADOLOGY

  • An trained intelligent voltage controller is developed to improve the transient responses of the voltage and frequency of an islanded microgrid with droop control
  • A trained Petri Probabilistic Wavelet Fuzzy Neural Network (PPWFNN) controller replaces the traditional controller in BESS for fast load shedding.
  • Islanded microgrid was developed ,integrating with Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) & Photovoltaic (PV) system.
  • Combines AI techniques:
    • Wavelet Fuzzy Neural Network (WFNN) for complex nonlinear dynamics.
    • Probabilistic Neural Network (PNN) for pattern recognition & adaptation.
    • Petri Net (PN) for decision-making & event handling.
  • The microgrid employs droop control for power sharing and frequency regulation​.A Power Prediction Wavelet-based Fuzzy Neural Network (PPWFNN) voltage controller was designed to enhance stability and load shedding
